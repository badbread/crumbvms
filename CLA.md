## How to sign

You don't email or print anything. On your **first pull request**, an automated
CLA check comments with a one-line statement to reply with; posting that comment
records your signature against your GitHub account for all future PRs. See
[CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md#contributor-license-agreement-cla).
