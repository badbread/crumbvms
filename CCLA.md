# CrumbVMS, Corporate Contributor License Agreement (CCLA)

> **About this document.** This is the **Apache Software Foundation Corporate
> Contributor License Agreement, Version 2.0**, adopted for CrumbVMS with only
> the party name changed ("the Foundation" → "the Project", defined below). The
> operative grant clauses (Sections 2 and 3) are **unmodified**. Adopting a
> standard CLA as-is like this is common practice and does not require custom
> legal drafting. The authoritative source text is at
> <https://www.apache.org/licenses/cla-corporate.txt>, if you want to verify
> this copy, compare against it. If CrumbVMS ever grants a third party a
> *commercial* license to the code (dual-licensing), have this reviewed by a
> lawyer first; it is the Section 2 "sublicense" right that makes that possible.
>
> **When to use this instead of the individual CLA.** If contributions to CrumbVMS
> VMS are made in the course of someone's work for an employer, or the employer
> otherwise has rights to the intellectual property the contributor creates, the
> **employer** should sign this Corporate CLA (in addition to each individual
> contributor signing the [Individual CLA](CLA.md)). This lets a company
> authorize a list of employees to contribute on its behalf.

## How to sign

[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) / the project's contact address. Each individual
